---

## Classification Key

- **Just me**: Tasks where I must own the judgment, responsibility, or physical action. AI cannot substitute for lived experience or accountability.
- **Delegate to AI with review**: Tasks where AI generates a draft, code, or plan, and I review every line before using it. Speeds up execution, but quality control is mine.
- **Collaborate with AI**: Tasks where AI is a thinking partner, tutor, or sparring partner. I drive; AI challenges, expands, or structures. Neither of us could do it alone.
- **Fully automate**: Tasks where AI runs end-to-end with a clear, repeatable rule set, and review is only needed for exceptions.

---

## Honest "Just Me" Tasks (at least two)

Tasks 3, 4, 5, 9, and 14 are marked **Just me** because they involve:
- **Accountability**: I am responsible for the outcome (APIs working, commits correct, accounts secure).
- **Verification**: I must see the actual screen, response, or bill to confirm truth.
- **Physical action**: AI cannot click buttons, accept terms, or pay invoices on my behalf.

This matters because claiming these are "automated" or "delegated" would be dishonest — the risk is mine if they fail.
